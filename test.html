Technical Approach ‚öôÔ∏è

To establish a robust baseline for the automated sorting of recyclable materials, we implemented AlexNet, a pioneering Convolutional Neural Network (CNN). This architecture was selected due to its historical significance in defining modern deep learning computer vision and its balance between computational depth and parameter efficiency compared to heavier models like VGG16.

Model Architecture Overview üß±

  • Five convolutional layers Conv2d designed to capture hierarchical features, from basic edges to complex textures found on glass and plastic surfaces.
  • Local Response Normalization (LRN)
  • Max-pooling layers were utilized to down-sample the spatial dimensions (224√ó224 input)
  • 3 fully connected layers with dropout
